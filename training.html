What We Offer

Our Training Programs

From fresh graduates to experienced professionals, we have a program tailored to your goals.

01 / INDUSTRIAL TRAINING

Industrial Training

Real-world, industry-focused technical training for students and graduates.

Our industrial training programs are designed in close collaboration with IT companies to ensure students acquire the exact skills demanded by today's hiring managers. Each program blends theory with intensive hands-on lab sessions.

  • Duration: 1 – 6 months, flexible scheduling
  • Live projects simulating real company workflows
  • Industry expert mentorship and code reviews
  • Completion certificate recognised by industry
02 / INTERNSHIP PROGRAM

Internship Programs

Structured internship opportunities to bridge education and employment.

Our internship programs provide students with direct, supervised exposure to professional IT environments. Interns contribute to real projects under the guidance of senior engineers, building meaningful experience for their CVs.

  • In-house and partner company internships available
  • Stipend opportunities for selected candidates
  • Letter of recommendation and internship certificate
  • Performance-based full-time job conversion
03 / CORPORATE TRAINING

Corporate Training

Customised upskilling programs delivered directly to organisations.

We partner with companies across Assam and Northeast India to design and deliver targeted training programs that upskill their technical teams. All programs are customised to the organisation's technology stack and business goals.

  • On-site or virtual delivery options
  • Custom curriculum tailored to your tech stack
  • Group training discounts and flexible batches
  • Post-training assessment and reporting
04 / CERTIFICATION PROGRAMS

Certification Programs

Globally recognised certification preparation for IT professionals.

Our certification programs are structured specifically to prepare candidates for high-value industry certifications. We provide study materials, mock exams, and expert coaching from certified trainers who have passed the exams themselves.

  • RedHat RHCSA, RHCE — Linux & DevOps
  • AWS Certified Solutions Architect
  • Microsoft Azure Administrator (AZ-104)
  • CompTIA Security+, Network+ preparation
