Professional IT Training in Guwahati

LUITECH Solutions is a premier IT training institute located in the heart of Guwahati, Assam. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to bridging the gap between academic education and industry requirements by providing hands-on, practical technology training.

We offer a comprehensive range of courses spanning full-stack development, cloud computing, data science, cybersecurity, and more — all designed and delivered by active industry professionals.
